About Me

Hi, I'm Shayan Memon

Over the past four years, I’ve specialised as a Gameplay Programmer, building 2D and 3D projects in Unity. My experience spans designing combat systems, responsive player controls, enemy navigation, and particle effects, with a focus on delivering smooth and engaging gameplay.
